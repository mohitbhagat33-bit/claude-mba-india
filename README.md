# Claude for MBA India 🇮🇳

Curated collection of high-performance Claude prompts and templates made for MBA students, Indian founders, and business professionals.

Built by Mohit (MBA 2026, IIT Bombay) to help the next generation of Indian leaders use AI 10× faster.

### What’s inside
- Case study analysis prompts (Harvard-style + Indian context)
- Financial modeling & DCF prompts
- Market research & competitor analysis
- Pitch deck & investor story prompts
- Startup due diligence checklists
- Go-to-market strategy frameworks for India
- UPI / GST / Razorpay / Indian regulatory prompts

All prompts are tested with Claude 3.5/4 and updated regularly.

### Why this exists
MBA coursework and early-stage startups in India move fast. These prompts remove the "how do I phrase this to Claude?" friction so students and founders can focus on strategy instead of prompt engineering.
